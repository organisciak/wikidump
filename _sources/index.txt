Installation
============
Prerequisites
^^^^^^^^^^^^^
 * `Natural Language Toolkit <http://nltk.org>`_
 * `Python-memcached <http://www.tummy.com/software/python-memcached/>`_ or `pylibmc <http://sendapatch.se/projects/pylibmc/>`_ (Optional but recommended)
